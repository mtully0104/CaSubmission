How it works

We focus on 4 Main concepts

1: Using our ranking algorithm, teams are placed into similarly skilled divisions.
2: No streaming or screenshots required. Track how your team is doing in real time with our live scoreboard.
3: Free and Paid Entry Tournaments to choose from.
4 : We run our cheat detection algorithms at the end of each tournament and ban players who violate our policies.
